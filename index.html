About The Vienna Standard

The Vienna Standard is an independent economic research publication exploring how markets, institutions, and incentives shape outcomes across different contexts. Founded by Jake, the publication examines economic questions through geographical case studies, theoretical frameworks, and policy analysis.

Named in homage to the birthplace of the Austrian School of economics, The Vienna Standard draws on insights from institutional economics, public choice theory, and comparative political economy to understand how economic systems evolve and function.
